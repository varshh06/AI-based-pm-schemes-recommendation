Welcome to PM Internship schemes recommendation platform

Benefits of PM Scheme Internships

  • Gain exposure to govt. initiatives & policymaking
  • Develop technical & soft skills
  • Boost career prospects & resume value
  • Contribute to nation-building projects
  • Earn recognition with govt. certification
  • Monthly assistance of ₹4500 by Government of India and ₹500 by Industry

Duration and Mode

  • Internship Duration: 12 months
  • Mode of Conduct: Online / Offline / Hybrid

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 21-24 years
  • Not employed full-time
  • Not enrolled full-time in education
  • Family income: less than ₹8 Lakhs Per annum
  • No family member in a government job
